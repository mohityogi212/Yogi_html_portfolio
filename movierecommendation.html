The Best Movies According to Mohit

My Top Movies of all-time.

Rocky

This is my favorite movie of which I have watch only some clips.

Sooryavansham

One of the best family movies which teaches you Indian Family ethics on which I resonate the most.

Narnia

When it comes to folktales which encompasses beautiful story, action, thrill and emotions I love this movie

Karate Kid

A classic movie that teaches important life lessons through martial arts.

Chennai vs China

An action-packed movie that showcases the power of friendship and unity.

Om Shanti Om

A movie that combines humor, drama, and spirituality in an enjoyable way.

Casper

A family-friendly movie that offers a mix of comedy, mystery, and supernatural elements.

Harry Potter

A magical adventure that captivates audiences of all ages.

Chandni Chowk to China

A comedy-drama that explores the journey of two friends searching for a stolen idol.

The Top Guns

An action-packed movie featuring a team of elite soldiers fighting against all odds.

The Martians

A science fiction film that tells the story of astronauts stranded on Mars, fighting to survive.

Marvel Movies

A collection of movies from the Marvel Cinematic Universe, offering a wide range of superhero adventures.

Aladdin

A Disney classic that tells the story of a young man granted magical powers by a mysterious lamp.
